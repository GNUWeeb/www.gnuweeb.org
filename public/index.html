Things you should know when joining GNU/Weeb:

  1. Use common sense. [3]
  2. Most of the members here are Indonesians, hence Bahasa Indonesia and English language are allowed. [1]
  3. No harsh words/slurs allowed, words like "fuck, shit" are okay within boundaries. [1] [2] [3]
  4. Don't spam stickers nor messages[2] [3]
  5. You can promote your groups/channels on Saturday/Sunday GMT+07, as long as it's SFW, not a crypto/scam, and NOT A WHATSAPP GROUP. [1] [3]
  6. No NSFW allowed except if you have already given permission or the admins are asking for it [2] [3]
  7. Don't send sketchy and/or spam links (for instance referral links) [3]
  8. Debate is encouraged.
  9. Be polite and have fun, you can talk about OOT stuff here (except KPOP).
  10. If you see someone is not abiding the rules, /report them and wait until admins come to handle.

Punishments

  1. Will get called out from admins.
  2. /warn, 5 warns = mute
  3. /tmute, /mute, /ban

GNU/Weeb Debate Ethics

  1. Make argument as casual and easy to understand as possible, so others can comprehend.
  2. Don't trash talk, it really isn't a strong argument.
  3. Be responsible for what you say.
